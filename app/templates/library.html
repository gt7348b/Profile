Transportation Planning Library

This represents a cross section of various planning studies that are not generally found online. It is not meant to be a comprehensive focus, but does present public materials with a focus on transit planning efforts in Atlanta, Georgia. It also includes the work products of the Transit Planning Board - a joint effort of the Atlanta Regional Commission, Georgia Regional Transportation Authority, and the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority from 2006 - 2008.
